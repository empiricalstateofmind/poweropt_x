Constant power is how many athletes train, however this doesn't always equate to the fastest times. By considering the forces at play over a segment, and the power profile of an athlete we calculate the optimal power profile to get the fastest times. Poweropt is a simple tool to see where your power is most effective on an effort.

  • Pick and Strava segment, rider profile, and wind conditions.
  • Find the optimal power profile and see the time savings beyond constant power.
  • Export power profile to practice indoors (or out!).
  • Go faster!

Poweropt is currently running as a standalone application (see the video below for a demonstration). If you have any queries or would like to trial Poweropt for yourself then please get in touch.
